# HOW TO USE LINGUAAI

## Free Chat Mode
Just type naturally and get corrections:
- "I go to school yesterday"
- "She don't like pizza"
- "We was playing football"

## Roleplay Mode
Say what scenario you want:
- "Let's practice a job interview"
- "I want to practice at a restaurant"
- "Can we do a travel scenario?"
- "Let's have a casual conversation"

The AI will roleplay as:
- Hiring manager (interview)
- Hotel receptionist (travel)
- Waiter (restaurant)
- Friend (casual)

## Quiz Mode
Ask for a quiz:
- "Give me a grammar quiz"
- "I want a quiz about past tense"
- "Test me on vocabulary"
- "Can I have a quiz?"

The AI will generate 3-5 questions for you to answer.
